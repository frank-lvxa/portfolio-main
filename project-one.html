Rogers Beyond The Seat

On January 23rd, 2025, I had the exciting opportunity to participate in the uXperience Design Jam, presented by Rogers at the Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business, University of Waterloo. The challenge tasked contestants with developing a brand strategy to enhance customer awareness of Rogers' relatively new initiative, the Beyond The Seat program.

Working alongside a talented team—including Hoy Wu, Naveena Krishnabagavan, Nia Brathwaite, and Hazel Olabode—we analyzed Rogers' audit data and identified a unique opportunity to elevate the program. Our team envisioned a transformation that would turn Beyond The Seat into a standout mobile experience, making it more engaging, accessible, and impactful for users. Through strategic thinking and collaborative problem-solving, we explored ways to refine the program’s digital presence, ensuring it resonated with audiences and delivered a seamless, meaningful user journey.
